VAAGDEVI HOSTEL MANAGEMENT

Vaagdevi Hostel has a beautifully built a campus where students are certain to feel at home. Currently, we are providing AC and non-AC rooms and a spacious dining space for all the hostel residing students. The food served for our students is of great quality, nutritious meals, and snacks. Our chefs use student surveys and feedback to create a tasty menu that is inclusive of different faith and dietary needs. The hostel facilities at Vaagdevi are subject to availability and would request students & parents to contact the admin office for further details. Vaagdevi Hostel management has comfortable hostel facilities on campus and off-campus for both Girls and Boys. These facilities are limited at present and hence allocated on first cum first-served basis. Total capacity for Girls is 100 and for Boys, it is 60. Vaagdevi Management has plans to expand its capacity in the coming years. The Facilities have both A/C and Non-A/C with attached bath and some common bath.

Vision of Vaagdevi Hostel Management

1. To provide an abode of ideal ambience through happy housing, nutritious nourishment, and caring counselling to prepare the youth to carve out their careers.
